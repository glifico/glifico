About Glifico
Glifico is a platform that allows translation agencies to contact translators and interpreters from all the world and translate documents into any languages.
Our Vision
We want to create a brand new way to communicate and collaborate faster and easier without geographical limits, increasing job opportunities and efficiency of the usual workflow.
Our Mission
To create a dynamic and interactive environment that allows allows agencies to find the best translators or interpreters for a specific project using a data validation system based on ratings and feedback.
